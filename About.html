About Us....

Welcome to Country INN and Suites by Carlson, where the charm of Carlson meets the comfort of home! Unwind amidst lush greenery, savor local delights, and experience warm hospitality that feels like family. Come, settle in, and let us create memories that will last a lifetime..

  • Country Inns & Suites was launched by Carlson Hotels (now Radisson Hotel Group) in 1986, targeting the "middle-class" market with cozy, affordable stays. It initially carried the simpler name "Country Inns by Carlson.
  • Country Inns & Suites was positioned as a more accessible brand compared to its upscale, full-service sister brand, Radisson Hotels.
  • Today, Country Inn & Suites by Radisson boasts locations across North America, Latin America, Asia, and Europe, offering comfortable accommodations for budget-conscious travelers.

Check In Process:

  • Guests will be required to fill in the registration card, either in a digital format or on paper.
  • The staff will welcome you and confirm your reservation details. You will be asked to sign the registration form and agree to the hotel's policies.
  • They will ask for your identification and may take a copy for their records.
  • If you have any questions about the hotel or the surrounding area, feel free to ask the staff.
  • Once the check-in process is complete, you will receive your room key or key card.
  • The use of online fund transfers or credit/debit cards is encouraged in order to minimize the handling of cash.
  • The staff may provide you with directions to your room or offer to escort you.

When Using Public Areas

Spa
  • Spa services will only be available to in-house guests and after same is permitted by the authorities.
  • There will be some limitations to the offerings.
  • Guests will be required to sign a disclaimer in keeping with Government and Ministry of Health regulations prior to obtaining services.
Pool
  • The use of the pool will be available to in-house guests only and there will be a limit to the number of persons entering the pool.
  • Guests will be provided pool towels which are sanitized and packed in individual packaging
  • Pool towels and sunbeds will be sanitized after each use. Guests are encouraged to drop off used towels at the designated place.
  • Sunbeds will be arranged as per request.
  • Chlorination and area disinfection of the pool will take place at regular intervals.
Gym
  • The gym may only be utilized by prior appointment.
  • All equipment will be sanitized before and after every use.
  • Towels will be laundered after each use.
  • Guests will be encouraged to sanitize their hands before and after using gym equipment.
  • A mat for sanitizing footwear will be placed at the entrance to the gym.
